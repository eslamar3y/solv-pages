Our Story

Built to solve. Driven to grow.

Solv was founded with a simple idea: businesses deserve digital partners who actually care about their results — not just deliverables.

We started as a small team of engineers and designers who were tired of seeing businesses get generic, cookie-cutter solutions that didn't move the needle. So we built Solv to be different.
