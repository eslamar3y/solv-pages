FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we get from new clients.

How long does a typical project take?
It depends on the scope. A standard website takes 2–4 weeks. Mobile apps typically take 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline during our first call.
What's the cost of a project?
Every project is unique. After understanding your requirements, we provide a detailed, transparent quote with no hidden fees.
Do you provide support after launch?
Yes — we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages. We stay with you after delivery to ensure everything keeps running smoothly.
Can you work with an existing design or codebase?
Absolutely. We can build from your designs, continue an existing codebase, or redesign and rebuild from scratch.
What industries do you work with?
We work with startups, SMEs, and enterprises across various industries including e-commerce, fintech, healthcare, education, and more.
